Frequently Asked Questions

What is Anonymous Hope?

Anonymous Hope is one of India's leading online personal fundraising and crowdfunding platform that enables NGOs to fund their programs as well as individuals to fund their medical expenses, creative projects and personal projects. Alternatively, it allows you to donate to a cause you support or, choose from a host of vetted causes here on Anonymous Hope.

Can I raise funds for someone I'm close to?

Yes, you can also raise funds for a friend, a loved one or anyone in need during life's crucial moments. You can start a fundraiser for a loved one and help raise funds for that individual.

How do I start a fundraiser?

You can create a fundraiser on Anonymous Hope in less than 5 mins! Click on the start a fundraiser button and from there we will contact you soon with the approval of your fundraiser and ask futher details.

Is Anonymous Hope Safe?

Yes, your fundraiser or the fundraiser you've donated money to have the very best in secure payment encryption technology. Not only are your donors online payments safe, your money is stored securely until you're ready to request a withdrawal via electronic bank transfer.
